Shipping and Handling

An initial deposit representing 50% of the total amount is required before the start of production of your clothing. We offer free shipping to all of our customers for orders of $400 (before taxes) or more, if the amount is less than $400 (before taxes) a $7.50 shipping fee will be applied for deliveries on the Island of Montreal, a fee of $13.50 will be applied for deliveries outside of Montreal.
